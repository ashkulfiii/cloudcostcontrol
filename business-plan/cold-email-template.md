## Subject Line:
Quick question about your Azure spend, [First Name]?

## Email Body:

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company] is scaling fast—congrats on the [recent milestone/funding/expansion]. 

Quick question: Are you seeing unexpected Azure cost spikes that are hard to explain to leadership?

Most finance teams we work with tell us their biggest headache is getting visibility into cloud spending across departments. Last month, we helped a [similar company size] reduce their Azure costs by 28% without touching production.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if we can do the same for [Company]?

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. Here's a 2-minute breakdown of how we saved [Similar Company] $47K/month: [link]

## Key Elements Used:
- ✅ Personalized subject line with company name
- ✅ Under 100 words
- ✅ Specific pain point (Azure cost visibility)
- ✅ Concrete proof (28% reduction, $47K savings)
- ✅ Clear CTA (15-minute call)
- ✅ P.S. with additional value
- ✅ Question-based opening to invoke curiosity
- ✅ No pushy language or multiple asks
