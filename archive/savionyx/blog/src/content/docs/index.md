## Welcome to Savionyx!

Hello and welcome to Savionyx! 👋 We are thrilled to have you on board. Savionyx is your go-to solution for managing and optimizing your Azure costs effectively. Our platform provides a comprehensive suite of tools to help you gain insights into your Azure spending and make informed decisions to save money.

### Complete the Onboarding Process

To get started with Savionyx, please complete the onboarding process. This will ensure that you have access to all the features and tools that Savionyx has to offer. The onboarding process is simple and straightforward:

1. **Enter your Tenant ID**: Provide your Azure Tenant ID to link your Azure account with Savionyx.
2. **Grant Access to Cost Data**: Follow the instructions to grant Savionyx access to your Azure cost data. This will allow us to provide you with detailed cost analysis and recommendations.

Once you have completed the onboarding process, you can start managing your Azure costs with ease.

### Features of Savionyx

Savionyx offers a wide range of features to help you manage and optimize your Azure costs:

- **Cost Analysis**: Get detailed insights into your Azure spending with our comprehensive cost analysis tools.
- **Cost Optimization**: Receive recommendations on how to optimize your Azure costs and save money.
- **Budget Management**: Set budgets and track your spending to ensure you stay within your financial goals.
- **Alerts and Notifications**: Get notified of any unusual spending patterns or budget overruns.
- **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports on your Azure spending to share with your team or stakeholders.
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate Savionyx with your existing tools and workflows.
