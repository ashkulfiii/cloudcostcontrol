# Cal.com Setup Guide

Simple setup for your CloudCost Control scheduling using Cal.com (free Calendly alternative).

## 🎯 Why Cal.com?

✅ **Free** - Unlimited bookings on free tier  
✅ **Open source** - Not locked into proprietary platform  
✅ **Professional** - Clean, modern interface  
✅ **Customizable** - Match your brand colors  
✅ **Reliable** - Used by thousands of businesses  

## 🚀 Quick Setup (10 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to **https://cal.com**
2. Click **"Get Started for Free"**
3. Sign up with `contact@cloudcostcontrol.net`
4. Choose username: **`cloudcostcontrol`**
5. Verify your email

### Step 2: Set Your Availability
1. **Timezone**: Set to your business timezone
2. **Working hours**: 
   - Monday-Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
   - Adjust as needed
3. **Buffer time**: 15 minutes between meetings
4. **Date range**: 60 days in advance

### Step 3: Create Event Type
1. **Event name**: "Azure Cost Optimization Consultation"
2. **URL**: `cal.com/cloudcostcontrol/consultation`
3. **Duration**: 30 minutes
4. **Description**:
   ```
   Free 30-minute consultation to review your Azure costs and identify optimization opportunities.
   
   What we'll cover:
   • Current Azure spending analysis
   • Quick wins for immediate cost savings
   • Custom optimization roadmap
   • Our service options and next steps
   
   Come prepared with:
   • Access to your Azure billing dashboard
   • Current monthly Azure spend estimate
   • Main cost concerns or questions
   ```

### Step 4: Customize Appearance
1. **Brand color**: `#667eea` (matches your website)
2. **Profile photo**: Upload your company logo
3. **Welcome message**: 
   ```
   Welcome! I'm excited to help you optimize your Azure costs. 
   Please select a time that works best for you.
   ```

### Step 5: Set Up Meeting Details
1. **Location**: Google Meet (or your preferred platform)
2. **Meeting title**: "Azure Cost Optimization - [Invitee Name]"
3. **Questions for attendees**:
   - Company name
   - Current estimated monthly Azure spend
   - Main cost optimization goals
   - Preferred contact method for follow-up

### Step 6: Update Your Website
The thank-you page already points to:
```
https://cal.com/cloudcostcontrol
```

Once you create your account, this will automatically work!

## 🎯 Best Practices

### Optimize Your Availability
- **Peak booking times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM-3 PM
- **Avoid Monday mornings** and Friday afternoons
- **Buffer time**: Prevent back-to-back meetings

### Professional Communication
- **Respond quickly** to Cal.com notifications
- **Send agenda** 24 hours before meeting
- **Follow up** within 24 hours after meeting
- **Keep meetings** on time and focused

### Continuous Improvement
- **Review analytics** monthly
- **Update availability** based on patterns
- **Refine questions** based on feedback
- **Test different** meeting lengths

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Calendar not syncing?**
- Check calendar permissions
- Reconnect calendar integration
- Verify timezone settings

**Not receiving notifications?**
- Check spam folder
- Verify email address in settings
- Test with different email

**Mobile booking issues?**
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check mobile internet connection
